Financial and technology shares led the rally, with Google’s parent Alphabet becoming only the fourth company to reach a $1 trillion market capitalization, as investors were encouraged by a batch of healthy U.S. economic data along with positive corporate earnings reports.“With valuations elevated, corporate America will probably have to do the heavy lifting to get stocks much above current levels.” In a good sign for the U.S. economy, most retailers posted higher sales in December to finish out the holiday season on a strong note.
