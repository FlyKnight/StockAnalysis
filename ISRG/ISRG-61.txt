SUNNYVALE, Calif., April 08, 2020 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Intuitive (the “Company”) (ISRG), a global technology leader in minimally invasive care and the pioneer of robotic-assisted surgery, today announced certain unaudited preliminary first quarter 2020 operational results.As the COVID-19 pandemic expands, unprecedented measures to slow the spread of the virus have been taken by governments and health care authorities globally, including the deferral of elective medical procedures and social contact restrictions, which have had, and will likely continue to have, a significant negative impact on our operations and financial results.
