By Stephen Nellis July 28 (Reuters) - On Wednesday, Apple Inc Chief Executive Tim Cook will face questions from U.S. lawmakers about whether the iPhone maker's App Store practices give it unfair power over independent software developers.Marc Fischer, the chief executive of mobile technology firm Dogtown Studios, said Apple's 30% commission felt justified in the early days of the App Store when it was the price of global distribution for a then-small company like his.
