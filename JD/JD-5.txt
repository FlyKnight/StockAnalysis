(Bloomberg) -- Tencent Holdings Ltd.-backed MissFresh closed $495 million of financing from backers led by China International Capital Corp., underscoring strong investor interest in online commerce and groceries during the pandemic.Investors including Tencent and private equity giant Primavera Capital are pitching in on a separate funding round that values rival Xingsheng Youxuan at $3 billion, people familiar with the matter have said.
