By Gabriela Mello SAO PAULO, June 23 (Reuters) - Latin American e-commerce group MercadoLibre will kick off operations in its new fulfillment center in Brazil's Northeastern state of Bahia in July, the company said on Tuesday, as more customers have been shifting to online shopping during the coronavirus pandemic.MercadoLibre's efforts to expand its logistics framework comes as competition intensifies in Latin American's largest market as local players boost their marketplace platforms while U.S. rival Amazon.com also bolster its operations.
