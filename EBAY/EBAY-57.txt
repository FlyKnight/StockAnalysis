(Bloomberg) -- Adevinta ASA used its small size and a savvy $9.2 billion cash-and-stock bid to its advantage this week, surprising bigger and better-known competitors to win a hotly contested auction for an EBay Inc. unit.Rivals -- heavy hitters such as Prosus NV, a $157-billion tech investment company created by Naspers Ltd., and private equity firms including Blackstone Group Inc., Permira and Hellman & Friedman -- saw Adevinta as a long-shot to win early on in the auction, according to people familiar with the bidding.
