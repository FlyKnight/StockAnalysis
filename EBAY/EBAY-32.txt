Debuting today on eBay's YouTube channel, the premiere episode features MLB pitcher Pat Neshek who invites fans into his vault and shares the history behind his incredible card collection.Neshek explains how he got his start, displays more than 50,000 autographed cards - including rare finds and his top-rated 1970 Topps set, and talks about how he used eBay to help him source 95% of his current collection.
