eBay Inc, an American multinational e-commerce corporation based in California, announced that it has agreed to sell its Classifieds ads business to Norway’s Adevinta for a total consideration value of $9.2 billion.We believe the combination of the two companies, with their complementary businesses, creates one of the most exciting and compelling equity stories in the online classifieds sector,” said Rolv Erik Ryssdal, CEO of Adevinta.
