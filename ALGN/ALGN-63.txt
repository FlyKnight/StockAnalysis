NEW ORLEANS, May 1, 2020 /PRNewswire/ -- Kahn Swick & Foti, LLC ("KSF") and KSF partner, the former Attorney General of Louisiana, Charles C. Foti, Jr., remind investors that they have only until May 1, 2020 to file lead plaintiff applications in a securities class action lawsuit against Align Technology, Inc. (NasdaqGS: ALGN), if they purchased the Company's shares between April 24, 2019 and July 24, 2019, both dates inclusive (the "Class Period").About the Lawsuit Align and certain of its executives are charged with failing to disclose material information during the Class Period, violating federal securities laws.
