NEW YORK, NY / ACCESSWIRE / April 29, 2020 / The Law Offices of Vincent Wong announce that class actions have commenced on behalf of certain shareholders in the following companies.Specifically, the complaint alleges that Silver Lake and its fellow defendants violated the Exchange Act by selling a block of Intelsat's shares while holding material non-public information, including the fact that the Company had met with the Federal Communications Commission (the "FCC") on November 5, 2019 to discussed the sale of spectrum controlled by Intelsat for future "5G" use (the "C-Band").
