"Valorant," the tactical shooter from TENCENT HOLDING/ADR (OTCMKTS: TCEHY)-owned Riot Games, is getting a lot of attention — and numerous esports organizations have already started investing in talented rosters.Top talent from other titles like "Counter-Strike" and Activision Blizzard's (NASDAQ: ATVI) "Overwatch" began retiring to pursue the new first-person shooter game.
