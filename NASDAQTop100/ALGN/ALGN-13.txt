Align Technology, Inc. (NASDAQ: ALGN), a maker of orthodontic products to fix crooked teeth and other issues, has seen a stock gain of more than 130% since March and has "gotten ahead of itself," according to BofA Securities.The Thesis: Align's stock has more than doubled since its March lows and is up 15% for the year, as investors appear to be buying into the company's long-term potential to take market share from traditional braces, Ryskin said in a Monday downgrade note.
