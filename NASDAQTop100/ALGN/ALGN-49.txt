SAN JOSE, Calif., May 13, 2020 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Align Technology, Inc. (ALGN) today announced that due to circumstances related to the COVID-19 pandemic and to prioritize the health and well-being of our stockholders, employees, officers, directors and their families, the time and location of the Company’s 2020 Annual Meeting of Stockholders (including any adjournments or postponements, the “Annual Meeting”) has been changed to 1:00 p.m. Pacific Daylight Time and the location has been changed to a virtual-only format.The proxy card previously distributed will not be updated to reflect the change in time and format but should continue to be used by stockholders to vote shares in connection with the Annual Meeting.
