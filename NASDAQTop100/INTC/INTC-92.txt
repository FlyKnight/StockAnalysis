The FAAMG stocks, as Goldman Sachs’ strategy team, led by David Kostin, dubbed the big five in a research note published this past week, have added more than one-third to their market values in 2020, during the sharpest recession on record and a pandemic.Real-time data, such as that on the population’s mobility—are people going out or traveling?—TSA checkpoint numbers, and OpenTable restaurant reservations, show “the spike in virus cases is indeed sucking the oxygen out of the robust economic recovery of the past two-and-a-half months,” writes Scott Anderson, the chief economist of Bank of the West.
