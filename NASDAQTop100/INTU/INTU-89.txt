Shares in Intu Properties crashed more than 35% on Wednesday, after the owner of some of Britain’s biggest shopping centers scrapped an emergency fundraising saying “extreme market conditions” had left it unable to raise its minimum target of £1.3 billion from nervous investors.It is also negative for companies trying to raise money as institutional investors will be very cautious towards putting cash into new stock when there is so much uncertainty over whether we’ve seen the worst of the market fall,” said Russ Mould, investment director at AJ Bell.
