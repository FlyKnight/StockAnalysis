Federal, state and local agencies embrace digital technologies to ensure seamless delivery of essential services There was a time in the public sector when working from home was a special privilege offered to a select few employees.While the speed with which they were required to enable it presented challenges, new research conducted by GovLoop on behalf of Citrix Systems, Inc. (NASDAQ:CTXS), reveals that a majority have risen to the occasion, leveraging digital technologies to keep their employees engaged, productive and safe and their operations running.
