SANTA CLARA, Calif., June 08, 2020 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Applied Materials, Inc. today announced that Teri Little has joined the company as senior vice president and chief legal officer.“I look forward to working alongside Gary, the legal team and groups throughout the company to pursue new opportunities and markets where Applied can bring unique advantages.” Prior to her 18 years at KLA, Ms. Little was with the law firms of Wilson Sonsini Goodrich & Rosati, as well as Heller Ehrman White & McAuliffe.
