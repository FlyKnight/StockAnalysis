Like a great many other events planned for the coming months, the International Consumer Electronics Show (CES) that had been scheduled to take place in Las Vegas from Jan. 6 to Jan. 9 will instead be held in an entirely virtual format."We concluded it is simply not possible to safely gather over 100,000 people indoors with a raging COVID-19 virus and no real hope for a tested and widely available vaccine by January," CTA CEO Gary Shapiro wrote in a LinkedIn post.
