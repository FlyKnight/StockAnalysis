By Katie Paul and David Shepardson SAN FRANCISCO, WASHINGTON D.C. July 29 (Reuters) - Facebook Inc Chief Executive Mark Zuckerberg stumbled at a congressional hearing on alleged abuse of market power on Wednesday, as lawmakers confronted the social media titan with damaging internal emails about the company's acquisitions.The committee presented fewer such exchanges to the other executives testifying at the hearing, which also featured Amazon.com Inc's Jeff Bezos, Alphabet Inc -owned Google's Sundar Pichai and Apple Inc's Tim Cook.
