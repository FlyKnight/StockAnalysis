Shares of Synaptics (NASDAQ:SYNA) have popped today, up by 8% as of 12:10 p.m. EDT, after the company announced yesterday evening that it will acquire DisplayLink."Several market trends such as work from home (WFH), bring your own device (BYOD) and office hoteling coupled with the growing need for multiple, high resolution displays in enterprises are driving demand for universal docking and casting solutions," Synaptics CEO Michael Hurlston said in a statement.
