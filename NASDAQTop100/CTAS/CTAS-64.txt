A new online survey commissioned by Cintas Corporation (NASDAQ: CTAS) revealed that 91% of U.S. adults feel that employers should supply anti-fatigue floor mats as a benefit for their employees who work long hours on their feet.With a Seal of Acceptance from the American Podiatric Medical Association, Cintas anti-fatigue mats are designed and constructed to ease the physical strain that comes with long periods of standing on the job.
