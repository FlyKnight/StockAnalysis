Cintas regularly examines its operations to determine the optimal structure for continued customer service and future growth, and as such has recently analyzed the Milwaukee market.Based upon this study, and unforeseeable business circumstances related to the COVID-19 pandemic, the company has made the decision to close its Menomonee Falls location at N51 W139915, Northpark Drive as of August 31, 2020 and transition servicing its customers and processing products to other local operations.
