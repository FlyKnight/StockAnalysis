LOS ANGELES, CA / ACCESSWIRE / February 7, 2020 / The Schall Law Firm, a national shareholder rights litigation firm, announces the filing of a class action lawsuit against Cintas Corporation ("Cintas" or "the Company") (NASDAQ:CTAS) for violations of 10(b) and 20(a) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 and Rule 10b-5 promulgated thereunder by the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission.Investors who purchased the Company's securities between March 6, 2017 and November 13, 2019, inclusive (the ''Class Period''), are encouraged to contact the firm before February 10, 2020.The Schall Law Firm represents investors around the world and specializes in securities class action lawsuits and shareholder rights litigation.
