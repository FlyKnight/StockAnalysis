Micron Technology Inc. shares jumped in after-hours trading Monday after the memory-chip maker said sales surged in the third quarter and are expected to remain strong in the final period of its fiscal year amid the COVID-19 pandemic.While the COVID-19 pandemic has been cited as fueling a boost in memory-chip sales, many analysts suspect that may have oversupplied the market, which could bode poorly for the second half of the year — but not according to Micron’s forecast.
