The stock is currently trading at a record high on expectations of increased use of Zoom’s video conferencing services and products due to the spread of coronavirus, which has been compelling people to stay connected with friends and family, and work from home.What’s more, the government’s recent move to gradually relax shelter-in-place measures has resulted in a record number of new coronavirus cases, which undoubtedly will compel people to spend a lot more time at home, something that bodes well for streaming services, videoconferencing platforms and dominant cloud players, to say the least.
