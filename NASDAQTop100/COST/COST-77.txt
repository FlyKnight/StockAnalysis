“After several months of unprecedented disruption across the business, a return to (near) normalcy has begun, a positive for the member experience” and Costco’s income, says analyst John Heinbockel.He said that from opening hours to product availability, “most items are largely back to where they were at the outset of Covid.” The removal of restrictions on purchases such as meat and toilet paper—as well as limits on shopper numbers—are good for Costco and its customers.
