With federal efforts under way to rescind certain content liability protections for internet platforms, Airbnb co-founder and CEO Brian Chesky said the tech industry can embrace the future — or have it force-fed.It’s an industry thing.” From Airbnb to Tripadvisor, Yelp, and Facebook, tech platforms have come under fire for evading many liability lawsuits over their content because of the Section 230 provisions of the Communications Decency Act.
