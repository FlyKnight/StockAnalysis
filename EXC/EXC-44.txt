Recognizing the additional financial strain many families and businesses are experiencing during the COVID-19 pandemic, ComEd also is voluntarily extending its suspension of disconnections and new late payment charges through at least June 1, 2020 – one month beyond the original moratorium announced March 13.ComEd’s high priorities include integrating more renewable energy into the grid, supporting the growth of electric vehicles and meeting demands among commercial customers, such as the growing data center sector, for superior reliability, power quality and competitive rates.
