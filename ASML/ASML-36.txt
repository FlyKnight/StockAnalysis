By Toby Sterling AMSTERDAM (Reuters) - ASML Holding NV, a key supplier to computer chip makers such as Samsung and Intel, reported worse-than-expected first quarter earnings on Wednesday but said customer demand for its products remains strong despite the coronavirus outbreak.ASML is the dominant maker of lithography machines, giant systems that cost up to 200 million euros each and are used by semiconductor manufacturers such as TSMC, Samsung and Intel to help make the circuitry of computer chips.
