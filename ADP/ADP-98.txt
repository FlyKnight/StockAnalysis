By Geoffrey Smith Investing.com -- U.S. stock markets opened sharply higher on Wednesday, as the revival of Joe Biden’s presidential campaign limited the risk of a radical left-wing administration under Bernie Sanders after November.The market took a more favorable view, on reflection, of the Federal Reserve’s emergency interest rate cut on Tuesday, seeming to accept that it was likely to reduce risks in the financial system and, as Fed Chair Jerome Powell claimed, provide a “meaningful boost” to the economy.
