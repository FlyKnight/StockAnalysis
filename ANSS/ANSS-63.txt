Two-year initiative supports rapid integration of software aligned to the FACE Technical Standard PITTSBURGH, March 12, 2020 /PRNewswire/ -- The United States Army, L3Harris Technologies and Ansys (NASDAQ: ANSS) are advancing the performance and affordability of next generation aviation and missile system applications.With emerging threats and limited resources, we simply have to provide more capabilities to our warfighters faster with less funding," said Joe Carter, U.S. Army Program Executive Office Aviation G10 Tactical Branch Chief and FACE Consortium Steering Committee chair.
