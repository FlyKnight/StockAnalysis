Kabbage, an Atlanta-based fintech, has lately positioned itself as an SBA lender for the PPP program, which aims to provide loans to small businesses struggling in the Covid-19 recession.“Banks have done the vast majority of PPP lending, but I wouldn’t underestimate the role that fintechs played in a short period of time,” said Scott Pearson, a partner at the Los Angeles law firm Manatt, Phelps & Phillips.
